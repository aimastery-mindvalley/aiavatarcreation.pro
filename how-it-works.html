The process

One recording session. Content from then on.

  1. You record once

    Photos and a voice for an avatar. Audio and a short video for a twin. We guide the entire session, from your home, no studio.

    This is the last filming day your content requires.

  2. We build your avatar or your twin

    Created from your photos and your chosen voice, or trained on your footage until the person on screen feels like you on a good day. You review the result and give the green light before anything else happens.

  3. Your words, written with you

    Scripts come from your frameworks, your teachings, your stories. You approve every word before your avatar speaks it. Nothing is published without your yes.

  4. Your avatar delivers

    Finished videos arrive ready to post. Your message shows up consistently, week after week, while your calendar stays clear for clients.
